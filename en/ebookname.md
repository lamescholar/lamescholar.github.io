**ebook name**<br/><br/>

On the Internet, e-books can often be found with a random title, which is inconvenient to store. Therefore, such books should be renamed. Personally, I use the rutracker standard for this:<br/><br/>

Nagel T. - What Does It All Mean__ A Very Short Introduction to Philosophy - 1987<br/><br/>

You cannot use a question mark, quotation marks, or a colon in the file name. You can use underscores instead.
