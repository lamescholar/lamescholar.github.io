**Book searching**<br/><br/>

Whenever I'm looking for a book, I check 4 sources:

<https://libgen.rs/>

<https://vk.com/docs>

<http://rutracker.org/forum/index.php>

<http://flibusta.is/a><br/><br/>

If the book is in English and it is not in these 4 sources, you can look at the Internet Archive. There are a lot of digitized books there due to the cooperation with American libraries:

<https://archive.org/><br/><br/>

The book can only be available for rental for an hour. The script helps here.

Look [Internet Archive](https://lamescholar.github.io/en/archive).<br/><br/>

If the book is not in all of these sources, last chance is googling it. If there is no book in Google, then most likely the book is not digitized.
