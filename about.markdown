I started this site to share tips on book scanning.

According to Steven Pinker, the poor are living better than ever. At the same time, the amount of hoarded wealth surpases the monarchy levels. The enclosure of economic assets rests not just on physical fenses. It rests on copyrights and licenses that force us to rent, subscribe and agree to terms and conditions. Unlike land, information has no natural scarcity. It can be copied, multiplied and shared infinitely. That's where it's called piracy and seized by the government. Information is much like the [Schmoo](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=yA9WPQeow9c).

This blog runs on [Jekyll](/en/jekyll).
<br><br>

We need to take information, wherever it is stored, make our copies and share them with the world.<br>
[Aaron Swartz - Guerilla Open Access Manifesto (2008)](/2024/07/14/guerilla-open-access-manifesto.html)

Мы должны брать информацию везде, где бы она ни хранилась, делать копии и делиться ими с миром.<br>
[Аарон Шварц - Манифест партизанского открытого доступа (2008)](/2024/07/17/guerilla-open-access-manifesto-ru.html)
